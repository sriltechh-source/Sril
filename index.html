Common Questions

FAQ

Everything you might want to know before we start working together.

How long does a typical project take?

A simple website usually takes 1–2 weeks. A full web application with a database and dashboard takes 3–6 weeks depending on complexity. I'll give you a realistic timeline before we begin.

What information do you need to start?

Just your business name, what you do, who your customers are, and what you'd like the website or app to achieve. I'll guide you through the rest — no technical knowledge required.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes — every project includes revision rounds so you can request changes before the final delivery. I want you to be genuinely happy with the result, not just satisfied.

Will my website work on mobile?

Absolutely. Every site and app I build is fully responsive and tested across phones, tablets, and desktops. Mobile-first design is a core part of my development process.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept UPI, bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS), and Razorpay. Payment is typically split: 50% upfront to begin and 50% on delivery. Flexible arrangements are possible for larger projects.

Do you provide support after launch?

Yes — all projects include a free support period after launch. Ongoing maintenance, updates, and new features are available as needed, billed separately or as a monthly retainer.
